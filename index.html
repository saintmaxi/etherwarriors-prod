ETHER WARRIORS

Ether Warriors are a group of 10,000 elite on-chain warriors conquering dungeons hidden on the blockchain! Warriors can be sent to clear dungeons and potentially undergo visual transformations that increase their success in future raids. The first 5,000 will be minted using Ether, and the remaining supply will be minted using a non-exploitable epoch-based system powered by the $RAID token earned from staking.
